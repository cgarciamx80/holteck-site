Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Holteck ("we", "us", or "our") operates holteck.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you fill out a contact or audit request form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name (optional)
  • Message content

We also collect standard analytics data through Google Analytics 4 (GA4), including pages visited, time on site, device type, and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry or audit request
  • To understand how visitors use our site and improve it
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

Form Submissions

Contact and audit request forms are processed by Formspree. Submitted data is stored securely and used only to follow up on your request. You can review Formspree's privacy policy on their website.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand traffic patterns. GA4 may set cookies in your browser to distinguish unique visitors. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

We do not use cookies beyond those set automatically by Google Analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Data Retention

Form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. Analytics data is retained per Google's default GA4 settings (14 months).

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you've submitted to us at any time by contacting us at connect@holteck.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at connect@holteck.com.
